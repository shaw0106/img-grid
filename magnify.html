Magnify App

Magnify is an app designed to improve your skills with building resumes, confidence and language skills that will help you get your dream job!
I used gamification to motivate the user into building skills that are otherwise dry and boring. By collecting badges the user is aware of their progress and gains the confidence in their own skills and can accuratly gage when they are ready to interview for their next job.
